Bot Commands

/list

Shows the last 5 free games found in memory.

/scan

Force a manual scan of all stores (Admin only, 15m cooldown).

/lang

Changes the bot's language for your server (Admin only).

/role

Sets a specific role to ping instead of @everyone (Admin only).

/toggle

Enable or disable notifications for specific stores like Steam or Epic (Admin only).

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get notified about free PC games?

Free Game Deals is an automatic Discord bot that tracks 100% free PC games. Add it to your server to receive instant alerts.

Does the bot track Epic Games and Steam giveaways?

Yes! Our game tracker monitors real-time promotions on Steam, Epic Games Store, and official giveaways on GOG.com.

How do I set up the free games Discord bot?

Click the invite button, choose your Discord server, and create a text channel named exactly 'free-games'. The bot will handle the rest.
